Frequently Asked Questions

Is Synaptimo Focus free?

Yes, the core features of blocking YouTube Shorts are completely free forever. We're building a Pro version with advanced analytics and features that will be available for a small fee to support development.

Is my data private?

Absolutely. Privacy is core to our philosophy. All statistics are tracked locally on your device. We never collect, transmit, or store your browsing history. Read our full Privacy Policy

What browsers are supported?

Currently, Synaptimo Focus works on all Chromium-based browsers including Chrome, Arc, Brave, Edge, and Opera. Firefox support is on our roadmap.

Can I disable the extension temporarily?

Yes! Click the extension icon and use the toggle switch to pause blocking whenever you need to. Your stats will continue tracking when you re-enable it.

Will this affect regular YouTube videos?

No, Synaptimo Focus only blocks YouTube Shorts. All regular videos, playlists, and long-form content work normally. You maintain full access to intentional, educational content.
